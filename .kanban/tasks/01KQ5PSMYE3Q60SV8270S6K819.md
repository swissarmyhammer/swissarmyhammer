## What

Final cleanup: physically delete the `<Focusable>` re-export shim and any remaining `Focusable` references after every per-component card has landed its visible-focus tests.

The architecture-fix card `01KQ5PP55S` left `kanban-app/ui/src/components/focusable.tsx` as a thin re-export of `<FocusScope>` so the per-component TDD loops would have a stable build. This card runs LAST in the chain — after every per-component card has migrated its imports to `<FocusScope>` directly and its visible-focus integration tests are green.

## Why this runs last

If we delete `<Focusable>` before the per-component cards finish, their TDD loops break: tests fail to compile, you can't write a red-green-refactor cycle, and the per-component work stalls. By keeping the alias alive until every per-component card is green, each card can:

- Switch its imports to `<FocusScope>` at its own pace, or
- Leave the imports referencing `<Focusable>` and let this cleanup sweep them later.

Once every per-component card is green and its imports have been audited, this card flips the lights off on the alias.

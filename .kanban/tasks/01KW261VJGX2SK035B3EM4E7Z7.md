If the production catalog is updated, the test data can silently drift out of sync, allowing faulty catalog entries to pass their golden test. Extract the catalog data as a module-level constant `const CATALOG_DATA: &[(Surface, &str, &str, &str, &str)] = &[...];` containing all six tuples, then reuse it in both `catalog()` and the test. This ensures the golden test always validates the production data and prevents drift.
